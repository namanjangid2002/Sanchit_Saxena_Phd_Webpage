About
I am a 3rd year PhD scholar in the Department of civil engineering under the guidance of Dr. Hrishikesh Sharma. My research work revolves around combining the two fields of study i.e., nuclear/ high energy physics and structural engineering for the development of a high strength radiation leakage-proof structure. My research interests are in the blast and impact analysis and resistant design, radiation effect on structures, risk assessment and reliability analysis.
